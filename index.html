About Us

Bringing Sweet Moments to Life

At Chef Brookie, we believe every moment deserves a little extra sweetness. Founded in 2024, we specialize in crafting delicious cakes, cupcakes, savory platters, and beautiful flower bouquets — all at prices designed to make everyone smile.

Based in Warsaw and serving Łódź and Lublin, our passion is to create fresh, flavorful experiences delivered straight to your door, fast and beautifully presented. Whether you're celebrating, gifting, or treating yourself, Chef Brookie is here to make your day a little more delightful.
